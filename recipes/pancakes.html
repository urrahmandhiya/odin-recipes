Pancake Recipe

Description

This easy pancake recipe makes light and fluffy pancakes. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, top them with your favorite syrup or fruit for a delicious meal.

What are Pancakes?

Pancakes are a popular breakfast dish made from a batter consisting of eggs, milk, flour, and baking powder. They are cooked on a griddle or frying pan until golden brown, then served with toppings like syrup, butter, or fresh fruit.

How to Make Pancakes

Making pancakes is simple: mix dry and wet ingredients separately, then combine and cook on a hot griddle or pan. The key to fluffy pancakes is not over-mixing the batter.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Maple syrup (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1

    In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.

  2. Step 2

    Make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.

  3. Step 3

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

  4. Step 4

    Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides. Serve hot with maple syrup or your favorite toppings.
