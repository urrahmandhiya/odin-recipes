Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

This classic Italian dish is a hearty and satisfying meal that combines ground beef, onions, tomatoes, and herbs to create a delicious Bolognese sauce. Serve over spaghetti for a perfect dinner!

What is Spaghetti Bolognese?

Spaghetti Bolognese is an Italian pasta dish made with a slow-cooked meat sauce. The sauce is typically made with minced beef or pork, onions, carrots, tomatoes, and wine. It's a comforting and filling dish.

How to Make Spaghetti Bolognese

Making Spaghetti Bolognese involves browning the meat, adding vegetables, and letting the sauce simmer to develop a rich flavor. The longer it cooks, the more delicious it gets.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.

  2. Step 2

    Add ground beef and cook until browned.

  3. Step 3

    Stir in diced carrot, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine, and oregano. Simmer for 30 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  5. Step 5

    Serve Bolognese sauce over spaghetti. Garnish with Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy!
